### Step 1: Login

Click the "Sign In" button on the bottom right of this page.

### Step 2: Download and Pin the Chrome Extension

Link: [Chrome Extension](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/oslyn/cedcabcpkkgdbjmikmlaobpgfeilfaam)

The extension currently supports the following platforms (as of Jan 2024):

- [Ultimate Guitar](https://tabs.ultimate-guitar.com)

### Step 3: Downloading a Song

Once on a supported platform, click on the extension.

Scroll down and click "Create"

After being redirected to Oslyn Tabs Song page, review the song. Click Save (bottom right).

## Chord Sheet Formatting Rules

- headers need to be in square brackets, example`[Chorus]`
- chords should be highlighted in purple. Should start with a letter in capitals (A to G), followed by decoration (m, sus, add4, et.)
